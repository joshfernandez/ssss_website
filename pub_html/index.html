About the SSSS

Hello, and welcome to the SSSS website! In here, you will find a variety of activities and events being hosted for Software Systems students and other students from Simon Fraser University's Faculty of Applied Sciences. You will also find biographies from the executive team and ways you can connect with them. So, feel free to look around.

Jun-Ho Kim

President

Year: 3rd

Past Roles: Internal Representative (May 2016 - April 2017)

Hey everyone! It is a pleasure to be elected president for the Software Systems Student Society and be tasked with representing all of you!

My goal for this student society during my term is to:

  1. Strengthen our relations with the other pre-existing DSUs within the Faculty of Applied Sciences,
  2. Display a stronger presence of Software Systems and the student society at the Surrey Campus,
  3. Make a clear distinction between Computing Science and Software Systems, and ensure that students are enrolled in the right program, and
  4. Bring fun and educational events for our Software Systems students and make sure their concerns are swiftly addressed.

If I'm not doing any volunteering work, you can find me either at the Fleetwood Recreational Centre or the SFU Burnaby gym! I also like to party and have a good time with friends when I'm not busy.

I hope that I can be a great president for you all. If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to contact me either through email, Facebook, or just along the hallways at SFU!

Best wishes,

KoreansWag
